FAQ

Common Customer Questions

How long will my decorating project take?
Project duration depends on scope and complexity. A single room typically takes 2-3 days, while whole house projects may require 2-3 weeks. We provide accurate timescales during quotation and stick to agreed schedules.

Do you provide all materials?
Yes, we can supply all paints, wallpapers, and materials at trade prices, passing savings to you. Alternatively, you're welcome to supply your own materials if preferred. We'll advise on quantities and specifications required.

Can you work around our schedule?
Absolutely. We understand the importance of minimizing disruption and happily work around your commitments. This includes working room by room, avoiding specific areas during certain times, or completing work while you're away.

Are you insured for working at height?
Yes, our insurance covers all aspects of our work, including height work up to three storeys. We use professional access equipment and follow strict safety protocols for all elevated work.

What preparation work do you undertake?
Proper preparation is crucial for lasting results. We handle all necessary preparation including filling, sanding, priming, and protecting surrounding areas. This ensures a perfect finish that stands the test of time.

Do you offer guarantees on your work?
All our work comes with a comprehensive guarantee. Interior work is guaranteed for two years, exterior work for five years, subject to normal wear and tear. We stand behind our craftsmanship completely.
